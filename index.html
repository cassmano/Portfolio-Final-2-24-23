About

Cassandra Manotham (she/her) is a Lao-American community worker, writer, learner, and technology enthusiast based in Philadelphia. In her work (and day-to-day) she is guided by compassion, curiosity, integrity, and service. She currently leads a digital equity program designed to serve the needs of immigrant and refugees communites.

Cassandra received her BA from Haverford College majoring in English and minoring in Environmental Studies with a focus on (post-, de-)colonial theory and science and technology studies.

Previously read: Gathering Blossoms Under Fire: The Journals of Alice Walker
